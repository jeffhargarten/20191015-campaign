Klobuchar's presidential campaign, by the numbers

By Jeff Hargarten
Star Tribune October 16, 2019

Since Sen. Amy Klobuchar's snowy Feb. 10, 2019 presidential annoucement, she's managed to survive a crowded field of Democratic primary candidates so far, qualifying for DNC debates at least until year's end.

Headed into 2020 though, she finds herself middle toward the lower end of the pack, her poll numbers and fundraising not yet resembling that of party frontrunners.
